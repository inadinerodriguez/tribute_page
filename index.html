Selena Quintanilla Perez

The "Queen of Tejano Music"

A Timeline of Selena's life and her devastating murder.

  • Selena was born on April 16, 1971 in Lake Jackson, Texas.
  • At the age of 10 Selena became the lead singer in her family's band Selena y Los Dinos. The band consisted of her brother A.B. on the bass guitar and her sister Suzette on the drums.
  • In 1987 at the Tejano Music Awards she won "Performer of the Year" and "Best Female Vocalist" for nine consecutive years after.
  • By 1988 she had released five LP records.
  • In 1989 she signed with EMI Latin records and EMI offered her a crossover album. She records three English-language compositions for the heads of her label.
  • Then on October 17, 1989 she released her self-titled debut album. She peaked on the US Billboard chart become Selena's first recording to debut on a national music chart.
  • In 1991 Yolanda Saldivar registered nurse and huge fan asked to start a fan club. Quintanilla, Selena's father, thought that was a good idea and said yes. Yolanda became close to the family as she became the fan club president.
  • In 1992 she marries Chris Perez. Chris was the lead guitarist for the band and they began secretly dating in 1990 because her father would disprove.
  • In 1994 she begins designing and manufacturing clothes. She opens two boutiques called Selena Etc. Yolanda managed both boutiqes after the family was impressed on how well she managed the fan club.
  • In the same year she also released her fourth studio album Amor Prohibido which debuted at number three on the US Billboard Top Latin Album charts.
  • Selena won "Record of the Year" at the 1995 Grammy's for Amor Prohibido.
  • After that win, her record label decided it was time she began working on and English crossover album. She started recording a few songs.
  • In this same year Selena's father Quintanilla discovered that Yolanda had embezzled more than $30,000 via forged checks from both the fan club and the boutiques.
  • On March 31, 1995 Selena met with Yolanda at a Days Inn hotel in Corpus Christi, Texas. There Yolanda pulled a gun and shot and killed Selena. Selena was just 23 years old.
  • In October 1995 Yolanda was sented to life in prison.
  • Selena's legacy lives on. She had movies and shows created about her. In March 2020, Selena became the first Latina artist to be inducted into the Star Trail of Fame.Her music lives on and she will never be forgotten
